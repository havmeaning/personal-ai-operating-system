---

## Long-Term Vision

The long-term goal of this research is to establish foundational design principles for Personal AI Operating Systems — systems that treat AI agents as first-class cognitive collaborators, running persistently on personal infrastructure to augment human knowledge work.

Milestones on the horizon:

- **2026 Q2** — Release PAIOS reference architecture v0.1
- **2026 Q3** — Community prototype and benchmark suite
- **2026 Q4** — Revised white paper incorporating peer feedback
- **2027** — Living review document (annually updated)
