## Research Questions

1. Can a six-layer modular PAIOS stack consistently deliver 2+ hours/week productivity gains across diverse knowledge worker profiles?
2. What is the minimum viable governance configuration for EU AI Act Annex III compliance in personal-scale deployments?
3. How can the SOUL format enable zero-friction PKG portability between LangGraph, MemOS, and Obsidian?
4. What are the measurable long-term cognitive effects of sustained agentic AI use in knowledge work?
